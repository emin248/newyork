About Us

Welcome to NJ Train Schedules, your streamlined companion for rail travel in New Jersey and New York.

Our Mission: We built this platform with one goal in mind – to make finding your next train as fast and frustration-free as possible. Unlike official apps that can be cluttered or slow, our interface is designed for speed, simplicity, and reliability.

How it Works: We utilize publicly available GTFS (General Transit Feed Specification) data to calculate schedules instantly on your device. We focus purely on the commuter experience: finding the next departure.

This project is an independent initiative and is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or sponsored by NJ TRANSIT®.

Schedule data is sourced from NJ TRANSIT's publicly available GTFS (General Transit Feed Specification) open data feeds. For official information, please visit www.njtransit.com
