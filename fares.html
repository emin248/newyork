How Much Does a Ticket Cost?

NJ TRANSIT® fares are based on the distance between your origin and destination stations. Prices vary for adult, child, senior/disabled, and student tickets.

Airport Surcharge

Newark Airport (EWR) +$8.25

A "Gate Fee" is automatically added to all tickets ending or starting at Newark Liberty International Airport Station. This fee covers the AirTrain connection.

Transfer Note

If transferring at Secaucus Junction, ensure your ticket states "Via Secaucus" to avoid fare disputes.

Popular Ticket Types

One-Way

Valid for a single continuous trip between specified stations. Non-refundable.

Monthly Pass

Unlimited travel for a calendar month. Best for daily commuters.

Senior/Reduced

Available for ages 62+, disabled riders, and military personnel. Usually 50% off.

Official fares may change. Always verify prices on the NJ TRANSIT® website or mobile app before purchase.
